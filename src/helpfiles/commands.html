Commands

Allowed commands in the Inputfield:

/topic - Sets the topic of the channel you are in, if you have the rights to do so.

/join channelname - Joins the channel channelname

/j channelname - Joins the channel channelname

/op nick - Gives op to nick

/deop nick - Removes op from nick

/voice nick - Gives voice to nick

/devoice nick - Removes voice from nick

/mode mode - Sets the specified mode

/away away-message - Sets you as away with away-message

/quit - Quits

/nick newnick - Changes your nick to newnick

/raw - Sends raw-message
